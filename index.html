About

I am currently a MSc student at the Mathematical Insitute at the University of Oxford, studying theoretical computer science.

Before this, I was an undergraduate student at Shandong University, where I earned a BSc in Mathematics, ranking 1/130.

I am interested in researching discrete, smooth and probabilistic structure, especially in algebraic and probabilistic combinatorics, information and manifold learning theory, and causal inference.

Current projects:

  • Limiting capacity of finite state channels
  • Study the case when feedback does not increase the capacity
  • Detect the non-e-positivity of spider graphs
  • Study the observational study with multi-valued treatment
  • Use Groebner and Graver bases to calculate opportunity cost matrices
